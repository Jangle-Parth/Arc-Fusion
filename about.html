Dr. Amitava Chaudary

Chief Mentor & Strategic Advisor

We are incredibly fortunate to have Dr. Amitava Chaudary as our mentor and guiding light. With over two decades of experience in technology and innovation, Dr. Chakraborty brings invaluable expertise to ArcFusion's journey.

"Innovation and tradition aren't opposites - they're complementary forces that, when combined thoughtfully, create something truly extraordinary."

Under his mentorship, ArcFusion has developed its unique approach to blending traditional Indian craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. His expertise in laser cutting technology and manufacturing processes has been instrumental in establishing our partnership with ATPL and developing our innovative production methods.
